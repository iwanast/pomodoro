About POMoDORo

POMoDORo let you decide on your own how long your focus-time should be to still feel well in the end of the day. Normaly a pomodoro is 25 minutes (created first by Francesco Cirillo) but we feel it should be up up to you what feels best in the end of the day. So try out different focus-times and settle for what feels good for you. (Settings are in the upper right corner if you did not try them yet.)

We recommend not more than two long-focus-pomodoro a day and the rest short ones. but even here, it is your feeling deciding what is best for you and your recreation.

And you can personalize more still by choosing between a lot of different themes.
